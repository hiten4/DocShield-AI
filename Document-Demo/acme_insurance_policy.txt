POLICY BENEFITS

This term insurance plan provides comprehensive life coverage for a period of thirty years from the commencement date. In the event of the policyholder's demise during the policy term, the nominee shall receive the full sum assured amount as a lump sum payment. The policy also includes an accidental death benefit rider that doubles the payout in case of death due to an accident.

CRITICAL ILLNESS RIDER

The policy includes coverage for the following critical illnesses:
- Cancer of specified severity
- Heart attack of specified severity
- Kidney failure requiring regular dialysis
- Major organ transplant
- Stroke resulting in permanent symptoms

EXCLUSIONS

The following situations are not covered under this policy:
- Death within the first year due to suicide
- Death due to participation in hazardous activities like skydiving or racing
- Death due to war or nuclear contamination
- Pre-existing conditions not disclosed at the time of application
